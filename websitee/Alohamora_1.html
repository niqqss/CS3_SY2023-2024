ALOHAMORA! UNLOCKING THE DOORS OF HARRY POTTER CHARACTERS

Harry Potter

Harry Potter, “The Boy Who Lived” and “The Chosen One”, is a young wizard characterized by his bravery and determination. Despite a challenging and abusive upbringing, he embarks on a journey of self-discovery as a wizard when he turns eleven. His years at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry are marked by the formation of deep friendships, especially with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. What sets Harry apart is his unwavering commitment to justice and his readiness to confront the evil forces and prejudice within the magical world. His loyalty to his friends is unwavering, and he fearlessly confronts danger, guided by a strong moral compass. These qualities have endeared him to fans, making him an iconic character. The central theme of the series revolves around Harry's transformation from an orphaned child into a heroic figure who ultimately triumphs over the dark wizard, Lord Voldemort.

  • Full name: Harry James Potter
  • Birthdate: July 31, 1980
  • Birthplace: Godric’s Hollow, England
  • Mother: Lily Potter
  • Father: James Potter
  • Siblings: None
  • Love interest: Ginny Weasley
  • House: Gryffindor
  • Blood-status: Half-blood
  • Personality: Brave, Loyal, Determined, Compassionate, Stubborn, Curious, Resourceful, Independent, Insecure
  • Occupation/s: Student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Auror, Hogwarts Professor, Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement
  • Boggart (magical creature that shapeshifts into whatever the observer fears the most): Dementor
  • Patronus: Stag
Fun Facts
  • His birthdate which is the 31st of July, 1980 is the same as the author, JK Rowling’s birthdate.
  • He was recognized as “The Chosen One” because he survived Lord Voldemort’s killing curse when he was just a baby.
  • His lightning scar in his forehead symbolizes his connection with Voldemort’s mind.
  • He was an exceptional Quidditch player and became the youngest Seeker in a century; he led many victories during his time.
  • His signature spell is “Expelliarmus,” a disarming spell.
  • He possesses one of the Deathly Hallows which was the Invisibility Cloak that was passed down through his family.
  • He is one of the “Golden Trio” that had a big role in the series.
  • He lost his ability to speak Parseltounge (language of snakes).
  • He is one of Voldemort’s Hocruxes.

Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger is a clever and resourceful witch known for her exceptional intellect and love of learning. She is a dedicated and loyal friend, often serving as the voice of reason among her trio of friends. Hermione's strong sense of justice and fairness drives her to stand up against prejudice and inequality in the wizarding world. Her unwavering moral compass, coupled with her courage and determination, make her an inspirational character. Hermione's character development includes her transformation from a diligent and rule-abiding student to a powerful and confident witch who plays a vital role in the fight against dark forces.

  • Full name: Hermione Jean Granger
  • Birthdate: September 19, 1979
  • Birthplace: London
  • Mother: Not mentioned in the movies/books
  • Father: Not mentioned in the movies/books
  • Siblings: None
  • Love interest: Ron Weasley
  • House: Gryffindor
  • Blood-status: Muggle-born
  • Personality: Intelligent, Curious, Resourceful, Hardworking, Courageous, Loyal, Sense of Justice, Empathic, Organized and Detail-Oriented, Inquisitive, Perfectionist, Independent
  • Occupation/s: Student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, possibly working in a high-ranking position at the Ministry of Magic or pursuing a career in magical law with her exceptional intelligence and dedication
  • Boggart (magical creature that shapeshifts into whatever the observer fears the most): Professor McGonagall telling her that she failed all of her tests
  • Patronus: Otter
Fun Facts
  • During her time at Hogwarts, she was a Prefect for Gryffindor and later becomes Head Girl.
  • Despite being born to muggles, she is often referred to as “The Brightest Witch of her Age” because of her intelligence and academic determination.
  • According to Rowling, she was actually thinking of making Hermione’s last name to be “Puckle.”
  • She adopted a very cute orange cat called Crookshanks, which nobody wanted for some reason but the cat actually turned out to be a half magical creature.
  • She had the time-turner as a gift for the reason that she could attend as many classes as possible so that she could learn more.
  • She was meant to have a younger sister but Rowling didn’t have enough time so she decided to let Hermione be an only child.
  • She is one of the “Golden Trio” that had a big role in the series.

Ron Weasley

Ron Weasley is a loyal and good-natured wizard who values his friendships and family above all else. He often provides humor and liveliness in challenging situations, contributing to the warmth and heart of the series. Ron's bravery, while sometimes overshadowed by his more famous friends, is undeniable, and he consistently stands up against dark forces and injustices in the wizarding world. Despite moments of self-doubt and insecurities about his abilities, Ron's unwavering loyalty to his friends and his strong moral compass make him an endearing and relatable character. His character development includes growing from a young and sometimes uncertain boy into a courageous and dependable adult.

  • Full name: Ronald Bilius Weasley
  • Birthdate: March 1, 1980
  • Birthplace: The Burrow near the village of Ottery St. Catchpole in Devon, England
  • Mother: Molly Weasley
  • Father: Arthur Weasley
  • Siblings: Bill Weasley, Charlie Weasley, Percy Weasley, Fred and George Weasley, Ginny Weasley
  • Love interest: Hermione Granger
  • House: Gryffindor
  • Blood-status: Pure-blood
  • Personality: Loyal, Brave, Humorous, Empathic, Insecure, Practical, Resourceful, Food Enthusiast, Honest, Determined
  • Occupation/s: Student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, working successfully at Weasley’s Wizard Wheezes with his brother George Weasley
  • Boggart (magical creature that shapeshifts into whatever the observer fears the most): Giant Spider
  • Patronus: Jack Russell Terrier
Fun Facts
  • Being the youngest son, his things are mostly composed of hand-me-downs including his robe and wand, but he later on got a new wand.
  • He is an excellent chess player which was very used in “Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone.“
  • He was a Keeper in Gryffindor Quidditch team and appointed as Gryffindor’s Prefect during his 5th year at Hogwarts.
  • His middle name “Bilius,” actually means angry or bad-tempered which really reflects his character.
  • The Hogwarts letter clearly stated “Students may bring an owl OR a cat OR a toad,” but he brought Scabbers, a rat.
  • He is one of the “Golden Trio” that had a big role in the series.
  • He has arachnophobia, a fear of spiders, which was clearly shown in the movies.
  • His least favorite sandwich filling is corned beef.

Albus Dumbledore

Albus Dumbledore is a wise and enigmatic figure in the wizarding world. As the headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, he is a symbol of knowledge, leadership, and moral strength. Known for his sense of justice and dedication to the greater good, Dumbledore is a powerful and respected wizard. He plays a pivotal role in guiding and mentoring the series' main characters, especially Harry Potter. Despite his past mistakes and regrets, Dumbledore is marked by his unwavering commitment to fighting against dark forces and his belief in the power of love and redemption.

  • Full name: Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore
  • Birthdate: Summer of 1881, exact date is not specified
  • Birthplace: Village of Mould-on-the-Wold
  • Mother: Not mentioned in the movies/books
  • Father: Not mentioned in the movies/books
  • Siblings: Aberforth Dumbledore, Ariana Dumbledore
  • Love interest: Grindelwald
  • House: Gryffindor
  • Blood-status: Half-blood
  • Personality: Wise, Kind, Sense of Justice, Brave, Leader, Mysterious and Enigmatic, Regretful and Guilty, Tolerance and Acceptance, Fatherly Figure, Love of the Greater Good
  • Occupation/s: Student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Headmaster at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Founder of the Order of Pheonix, Chief Warlock of the International Confederation of Wizards, Head of the Transfiguration Department at Hogwarts, Author of several important works on magic and magical theory
  • Boggart (magical creature that shapeshifts into whatever the observer fears the most): Corpse of his Sister
  • Patronus: Phoenix
Fun Facts
  • He understood many magical creatures by knowing different languages including Parseltounge, Mermish, and Gobbledygook.
  • He can do both silent and wandless magic.
  • Fawkes the Phoenix, shared a deep connection with him. It provided him one of its feathers which was used in Harry’s wand.
  • Rowling considered him as gay and is rumoured to be in love with Grindelwald.
  • His first teaching job at Hogwarts was as Professor of Defense Against the Dark Arts.
  • He has the ability to turn invisible, even without the invisibility cloak.
  • He is a fond of lemon drops and is often seen enjoying them.

Lord Voldemort

Lord Voldemort, born Tom Riddle, is a wicked and power-hungry dark wizard. His character is marked by a ruthless pursuit of power and a complete lack of empathy or compassion. Voldemort is a master of dark magic and is known for his ability to inspire fear and control his followers, known as Death Eaters. His obsession with blood purity and the desire to eliminate all Muggles and Muggle-borns (wizards with non-magical parents) from the wizarding world drive his actions. Voldemort's ultimate goal is to achieve immortality and domination over all.

  • Full name: Tom Marvolo Riddle
  • Birthdate: December 31, 1926
  • Birthplace: Wool’s Orphanage in London, England
  • Mother: Merope Gaunt
  • Father: Tom Riddle Sr.
  • Siblings: Not mentioned in the movies/books
  • Love interest: None
  • House: Slytherin
  • Blood-status: Half-blood
  • Personality: Ambitious, Cunning, Ruthless, Lack of Empathy, Arrogant, Feared, Hated, Obsession with Immortality, Manipulator, Paranoid, Self-preservative
  • Occupation/s: Student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Dark Wizard, Dark Lord
  • Boggart (magical creature that shapeshifts into whatever the observer fears the most): His Own Corpse
  • Patronus: None
Fun Facts
  • He created seven Hocruxes (his diary, Marvolo Gaunt’s ring, Salazar Slytherin’s Locket, Helga Hufflepuff’s cup, Rowena Ravenclaw’s diadem, Nagini the snake, and Harry Potter himself) to achieve immortality.
  • He fears death, therefore doing everything he can to achieve immortality.
  • He is actually a direct descendant of Salazar Slytherin (founder of the House Slytherin) through the Gaunt family which is possibly the reason why he can speak Parseltounge.
  • Albus Dumbledore was the only wizard he feared.
  • He was only 16 when he first created a Hocrux.
  • The lack of his nose is the result of using dark magic in splitting his souls into pieces when he was making Hocruxes.

Severus Snape

Severus Snape is a character marked by his complexity and moral ambiguity. He is a talented and skilled wizard with a mysterious and enigmatic personality. Snape's character is defined by his complicated past and his choices, often motivated by love, regrets, and a sense of duty. Despite his harsh exterior and at times questionable actions, Snape is a character with a sense of hidden nobility and a deep commitment to protecting those he cares about. Snape's portrayal throughout the series challenges readers' perceptions and adds depth to the narrative.

  • Birthdate: January 9, 1960

  • Boggart (magical creature that shapeshifts into whatever the observer fears the most): Corpse of Lily Potter

  • He was one of Slughorn’s favorite students during his time at Hogwarts.
  • When he was a student, he created several spells.
  • He is highly skilled in potions.
  • He possesses Legilimens (magical ability to read minds), which he mostly used with Harry Potter.
  • He’s one of the two people who managed to learn how to fly without the use of broomstick.
  • He was a double agent who appears to serve Voldemort while secretly working to protect Harry and support Dumbledore in their fight against the Dark Lord.
